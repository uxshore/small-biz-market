Vendor Application 4/27/24


The Small Business Market is happening on Saturday, April 27 from 11am-4pm at the Sherwin WIlliams Paint parking lot: 435 S University Ave, Provo, UT 84601.

As a vendor you will need to arrive early to set up your booth. You will have about a 10x10 foot space. This can include tables, chairs, a canopy, clothing racks, etc. Tables are not provided, so bring your own booth set up. If you check on Plan It rentals or KSL, there are plenty of options to rent a table for a day!

After filling out this form, I will send you an email! There is a $25 vendor fee that can be paid via Venmo
